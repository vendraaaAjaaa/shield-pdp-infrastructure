# Shield-PDP Stage 7 - Service Mesh And Zero Trust

The `zero-trust-mesh` service models identity-aware routing, strict mTLS posture, service identity review, and policy-based service communication.

Modeled controls:
- mTLS status and certificate rotation simulation.
- Service-to-service decisions: allow, deny, read-only allow, and approval required.
- Observable policy evaluation events for replay and SOC correlation.
- Trust-boundary views for tenant, vendor, devops, identity, and observability relationships.

No real certificates are issued and no live network enforcement is changed by the simulation.
