# Shield-PDP Stage 6 - Executive Reporting

Executive reporting summarizes technical simulation outcomes for CISO-level review.

## Service

| Route | Data |
| --- | --- |
| `/intelligence/api/executive` | attack impact, blast radius, coverage score, incident summary |
| `/coverage/api/executive-report` | detection SLA, coverage score, blindspots, services with telemetry |

## Metrics

- attack impact score
- blast-radius estimate
- detection coverage score
- blindspot count
- incident timeline availability
- detection SLA statement

All metrics are derived from synthetic lab telemetry and are not production risk measurements.
